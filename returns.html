Returns & Refund Policy

At Kuvala, customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, here's everything you need to know about returning items.

Effective Date: July 17, 2026

Eligibility for Returns

You may request a return within 7 days of receiving your order, subject to the terms below. To qualify for a return:

  • The return request must be made within 7 calendar days from the date your order was delivered.
  • The item must be unused, unworn, and in its original condition.
  • The item must be returned with all original packaging, tags, manuals, accessories, and proof of purchase.
  • Items damaged due to misuse, negligence, or normal wear and tear are not eligible for return.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective:

Perishable goods
Personal care & hygiene products
Underwear & intimate apparel
Gift cards
Digital or downloadable items
Customized or personalized products
Clearance or final-sale items

Damaged, Defective, or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact Kuvala within 48 hours of delivery. Include:

  • Your order number
  • Clear photos or videos of the item
  • A brief description of the issue

✓ Once verified, we will arrange a replacement, exchange, or full refund at no additional cost to you.

Refunds

After we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you of the outcome. If approved:

  • Refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5–10 business days, depending on your payment provider.
  • Shipping charges are generally non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Return Shipping

  • If the return is due to a Kuvala error (wrong, damaged, or defective item), Kuvala will cover the return shipping cost.
  • For all other eligible returns, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless otherwise stated.

How to Request a Return

1

Contact Customer Support

Reach out via our official WhatsApp Business account or email with your order details.

2

Provide Order Details

Share your order number and the reason for the return. Photos or videos may be required.

3

Wait for Approval

Wait for return approval and instructions before sending the item back. Unauthorised returns may not be accepted.

⚠ Returns sent without prior authorization may not be accepted.
